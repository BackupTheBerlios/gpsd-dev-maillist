As for the timekeeping, I did an experiment recently with a Garmin 18x
LVC (3.70) unit to find out what are the hw limitations. I've measured
the offset between PPS and timestamps captured on the first read() of
the message batch in NMEA mode (i.e. start of the RMC message).

http://mlichvar.fedorapeople.org/tmp/18x_nmea2.png

The x-axis is couple hours. It seems the NMEA offset is stable only to
150 ms.
