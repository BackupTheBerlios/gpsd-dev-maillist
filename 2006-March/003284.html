there's some stuff i'm hacking on where it would be really handy to
have ECEF position. sirfmon can obviously extract this information,
and i suspect other protocols make it available as well. i'm
considering hacking up a new command to give you ECEF position and
speed. Where the source protocol doesn't support such quantities, we
just return "?" - no sense in running the lossy transform twice. i
think there a couple of letters left that i can use...

for your amusement, attached is a screenshot of what happens when
you're trying to use an SS3 inside a cruise ship. the receiver is
pretty aggressive about hanging on to the signal, but that can lead to
some interesting results. i guess i really was going madly off in all
directions. :) +/- 221mph on the water indeed!
