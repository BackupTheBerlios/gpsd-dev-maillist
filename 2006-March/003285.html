> have ECEF position. sirfmon can obviously extract this information,

> considering hacking up a new command to give you ECEF position and

This info is often available in a separate packet type, that you have
to enable during receiver initialisation.   In most drivers the init
is hardcoded.  You would need some extra function in the drivers that
tells what packet types are to be enabled.
