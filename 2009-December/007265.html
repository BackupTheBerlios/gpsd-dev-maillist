> Eric,
> I just downloaded the tar ball and it built the rpm with "make dist-rpm".  However, the binary does not work.  The W and R commands work correctly but the "?WATCH={"enable":true};" does not.  It just hangs after spitting out the DEVICES and WATCH objects.  VERSION->rev is wrong as well.
> Mike

You probably want ?WATCH={"enable":true,"json":true};

There was a late change to support gpspipe where I introduced a json 
policy flag. If the documentation is misleading about this, please
point me at where so I can fix it.

For comparison,

    ?WATCH={"enable":true,"nmea":true};

is how you enable pseudo-NMEA dumping, and

    ?WATCH={"enable":true,"raw":2};

is how you enable raw binary dumping.
