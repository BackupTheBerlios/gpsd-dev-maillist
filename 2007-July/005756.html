Hi there,

   just a quick note to let you know I've knocked up a Qt-based xgps.

   For those of you who might be interested, a very early version can be
found at http://www.nippur.net/survey/gpsd/  Both source and RPM (i586)
files are provided.

   This is my first foray into Qt, and my first contact with C++ after
over 15 years, and anyway, I'm not a very good programmer to start with.

   Having said that, any comments/criticisms and especially, bug fixes
are all welcome.

   Note: you may need a recent version of libgps.so.16 for it to run.  I
don't think the libgps.so that comes with the 2.34 gpsd RPM is recent
enough.

Regards,
Diego Berge.
