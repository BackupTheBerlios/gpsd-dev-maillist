After upgrading to Ubuntu 9.10 (Karmic Koala) I have discovered that
libtool got completely broken sometime before 2.2.6.  The symptom is
that a clean build with autogen.sh fails; a workaround is to specify
the --disable-shared option.

This is on top of an earlier problem where libtool was spuriously
generating a libgps-0.0.0 rather than inserting the major and minor 
version correctly.

I have reached my disgust limit with autotools.  I will be moving 
us to scons as soon as is practicable.
