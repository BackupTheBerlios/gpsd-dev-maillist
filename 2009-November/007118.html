The build does not work on NetBSD, due to what I think are unportable
assumptions about ncurses.

On linux, which of curses/ncurses headers and libs exist, and where are
they?

On NetBSD, the following is in base (traditional BSD curses, plus wchar):

  /usr/include/curses.h
  /usr/lib/libcurses

and optionally via pkgsrc

  /usr/pkg/include/ncurses/ncurses.h
  /usr/pkg/lib/libncurses{,++}

in /usr/pkg/include/ncurses there is:
