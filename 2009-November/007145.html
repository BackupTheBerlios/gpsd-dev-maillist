GPSD Folks,
If you restart the deamon and then quickly execute a ?WATCH={"enable":true}; You get the DEVICES object before the DEVICE has had a chance to get the flags or subtype properties.  I also think the WATCH object should come before the the DEVICES object in the return.
Mike (mrdvt92)
