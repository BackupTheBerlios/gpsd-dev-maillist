I now think Rob Janssen's theory was correct and we got caught by the Berlios 
admins going on vacation.  That being the case, I'm divided in my mind over
whether to jump to Google.

The problem, of course, is that moving would both involve a lot of
administrative hassle and break everybody's links to the site.  Not
a thing to be done casually -- or at all, unless we think Berlios is
in collapse.

Rob's correct prediction has lowered my estimate of the odds that Berlios
is collapsing.  Thus, I now think watchful waiting is called for, as opposed
to an immediate move.

I don't hold this evaluation strongly.  Advice from senior developers, or
anyone who knows facts on the ground at Berlios, would be welcome.
