Here's what I've found so far:

Problem: RTCM104 requires one of sirf,tsip,earthmate,italk,garmin,evermore
Solution: Disable RTCM104 in configure unless at least one of these is
enabled. Allow compilation to fail due to no protocols being selected.
Let the test in gpsd.h enforce this.

Problem: TNT requires NMEA.
Solution: Allow configure to enable NMEA and continue the build
Alternate: Treat TNT as a subset of NMEA and required that NMEA be
explicitly selected in order to compile. Let the test in gpsd.h
enforce this.

Comments?
