> So let's think about that default.  The reference installation I

>
> My Thinkpad X40 has two USB ports, which I think is typical these

things, I'll sometimes hang 4 receivers off a hub. And sometimes I'll
throw a CF receiver into the mix. Two receivers is probably a
reasonable default, provided it's easy to crank up the max.

knows what time it is; a man with two watches is never quite sure."
