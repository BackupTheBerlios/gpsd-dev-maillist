No idea why this isn't working, but it seems that on the remove event I 
cannot filter on SYSFS{idVendor} or SYSFS{idProduct} or else my rule 
doesn't match.  Removing both of those so that the remaining match is 
mainly on KERNEL==ttyUSB[0-0]* and everything is working

Not ideal, but perhaps someone here any thoughts on why udev  behaves 
like that? (udev 141)
