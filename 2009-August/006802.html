least gpsd-2.39 does not compile with uclibc 0.9.30.1 (and probably any

        sed -i -e "s/finite(/isfinite(/" \
                gpsd.c gpsd.h-tail libgpsd_core.c

appears isfinite() is definited in glibc?)
