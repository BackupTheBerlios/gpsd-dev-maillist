> sentences should be. Should gpsd 'learn' what set of sentences is is

By "fun", you appear to mean "having the potential for many delightful bugs".

:-)

I recognize that trying to be adaptive in this way is often a recipe
for subtle problems and fluky bugs if things don't happen in the sequences 
we expect.  However, in this case I don't really think we can get in much
trouble -- the protocol is too primitive for that.

In truth, I'm a bit more put off by the thought that holding this bit of state 
would break binary compatibility of the gpsd_t structure *again*.  But that's
a minor issue.
