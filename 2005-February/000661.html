After having used this with roadgps, I can confirm that it is a good
idea.  It make it possible to leave xgps running for a long time, even
if I have to restart or replace gpsd on the fly.

seriously believe the clients architecture need an upgrade.

I'm aware of the problems with the current design, as I tried to
rewrite it to get it to reconnect.  It is doable, but require quite a
lot of rewriting.
