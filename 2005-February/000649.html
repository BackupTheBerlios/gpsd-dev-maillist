Your GPS just rebooted. Maybe it was a snap start so you didn't lose your
fix, but that's what the firmware kicks out on boot.

As for the "partial satellite data" business, I'd guess that it's something
related to downloading the almanac or ephemeris. That's a custom ugly hack
in your firmware though, as the four versions I have say nothing about partial
anything.

It seems that you're running an "ES" or enhanced sensitivity firmware. It
makes a speed-for-clinginess tradeoff: longer sampling, integration and
FFT search times to find and hang on to weak signals. Obviously this delay
will impose a minor navigational performance penalty, but nothing to get
seriously bent about. Just don't use it for the sole source of navigation
on your new F-18.
