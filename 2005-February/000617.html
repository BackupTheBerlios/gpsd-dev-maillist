But wait!  There's more!

The version of gpsprobe I just now checked in doesn't merely detect
SiRF devices, it ships them a control string to try to flip them into
NMEA mode (telling you that it's trying this).

If I have understood the specification correctly and not screwed up, the
new version of gpsprobe will get NMEA sentences out of your Leadtek.  From
there to hacking gpsd to support your device directly would be a short
and easy step.
