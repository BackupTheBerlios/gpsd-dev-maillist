I want to get rid of the -T and -s switches and have gpsd
autoconfigure itself by probing the GPS. I've already taken a large
step in this direction by teaching gpsd to hunt the right baud rate.

There are several reasons for this.  One is that, given hotplug
devices, we can't count on the GPS type staying the same during the
lifetime of a gpsd instance.  Therefore, we really should autoprobe
every time the device is opened.

Right now gpsd automatically recognizes SiRF-II chipsets, the
TripMate, and the EartMate.  That actually gets us most of the way
there.  Can we come up with probes for the FV18 and Garmin?
