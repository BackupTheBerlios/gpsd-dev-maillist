Ok.

The ais_json.i and gpsd_config.h are for mingw. The ais_json.i is a copy of
the outcome of a configure on linux. The gpsd_config.h is a static config
specific to mingw.  They are created in <gspd>/libQgpsmm/mingw.
I have to do it that way because there is no autotool on mingw (or only
under msys, but it's messy).
