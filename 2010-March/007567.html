I'd have a problem with github, because despite its coolness it fails
three of my tests for a new hosting site.  

One: it's a proprietary platform.  

Two: I could not count on being able to reach admins in real time.

Three: I want to pick one of the sites where the admins will be 
impressed as hell that they're dealing with "ESR". 

I guess the third point needs explanation.  If we ever *have* to
invoke the admins, I want them to be eager and concentrated on solving
our problem, not in a dismissive WTF-it's-just-another-user mode.
Since I've got this fame thing, it would be silly not use it to
maximize our chances of getting decent service.
