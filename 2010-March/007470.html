> I have only one comment on this:
> 
> Eric Raymond wrote:
> >1. The motivation: bring back polling
> 
> +1 for this.  I have an application that requires a positional fix
> every hour.  I don't even want to hold a GPSD connection open
> constantly, nor do I want to go into watcher mode.  I just want to
> connect, poll once for the latest positional data, then go back to
> sleep.

POLL won't necessarily solve your problem directly. The use case for it is when
the GPS is constantly open, so it's warmed up and always has a cached fix 
available.

There's a problem with real one-shot polling, with the device off or in low
power between opens, that is fundamental and can't be solved in
software - after you wake up the device, there's a variable delay
(which for a cold start can be up to 15 minutes!) before you get fix.  This
is why I scrapped the old polling-based interface.

I need to write an interfacing guide...
