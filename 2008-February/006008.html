Greetings,

    this is to let you know I have moved the home page for my Qt-based 
xgps clone to <http://www.navlost.eu/survey/gpsd/>.  This was after my 
previous hosting service went dead.

    For those interested, I have created RPMs of the University of Texas 
GPS Toolkit (GPSTk) v1.4 for a number of distros.  They can be found at 
<http://download.opensuse.org/repositories/home:/licehunter/>.  Note: 
the GPSTk home page is at <http://www.gpstk.org/>.

    Lastly, something which may come in useful in a pinch to those of 
you who need to do the odd coordinate conversion/transformation on a web 
project (or elsewhere) and don't have the php-mapserver extension 
installed.  As a last ditch resource, you may use the following URL for 
coordinate transformations in your scripts:

<http://www.navlost.eu/survey/srv/proj>

    The above will take your coordinates and projection info on the URL 
query part and will return a very simple XML or CSV file with your 
results.  See <http://www.navlost.eu/survey/srv/proj-man> for details.

Regards,
Diego Berge.
