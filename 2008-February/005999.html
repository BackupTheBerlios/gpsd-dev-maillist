Seriously, we didn't intentionally turn down any idle limits. I'm
thinking that it  might be some "optimization" in your TCP stack.
Rummage through your net.* sysctls and see if there's an idle timer in
there you can turn up, or TCP keepalives you can turn on.

Possibly run gpsd and/or telnet under gdb to see if they're catching
any signals that would cause a connection tear-down.
