Is anyone looking into adding Delorme EarthMate PN-40 support to GPSD?
 From what I can see from searching the archives and SVN it's not
supported currently and no one appears to be working on it but I
thought I'd ask anyway...

What I know so far is that it's appearing as a HID device in "data
exchange mode".  It also has modes where either the internal flash or
the SD card are presented as USB mass storage devices.  It also
appears to have a similar mode for uploading firmware.  Here's the
output of lsusb when it's connected in data exchange mode:
