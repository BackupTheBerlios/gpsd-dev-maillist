I'm not sure that "don't work" is the right term.  It's more a style thing.

I stick with Fedora because I "grew" up on it (well RH and SunOS too) and I'm surrounded by it at the office.  I personally like how there are more ready to go packages for Debian especially in the entertainment arena.  But, as y'all probably know, I'm strictly speaking a Perl Wonk and it runs on them all.
