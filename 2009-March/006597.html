Definitely not a gpsd problem here, sounds more like the cross-build environment
does not suit your real hardware. Does the hardware come with a (soft) FPU, if
so, does the FPU support divides? There's also a change that you run into
http://gcc.gnu.org/bugzilla/show_bug.cgi?id=16144 in case you're using gcc 3.4
Best is you ask somebody who knows your hardware and the system which is running
on it, otherwise you'll get nothing but guesses :)
