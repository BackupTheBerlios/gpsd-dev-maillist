Also, I have two requests:

1. You're the PPS expert we've got.  Would you please review
tjackson's patches?  If they're good, I want them in 2.39.

2. Lower priority - Recently I extended the driver interface so gpsmon
would be able to snoop into the driver structures and look at whatever
message was assembled and sent by the last driver methgod called.

For this to work, the low-level function in each driver that assembles
and ships commands needs to use the common assembly buffer,
session->msgbuf, and leave the total length of the message in
session->msgbuflen.

All drivers currently do this, except the Garmin one. Would be nice
if you could fix it to do that.
