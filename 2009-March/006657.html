Lukasz Stelmach pisze:

> I'll measure the power consumption today.

Here you are the hard numbers:

An average current during 2 minutes run with a fix:

1. Normal operation as a receiver, BT on, LOGGER on:     61mA
2. Normal operation as a receiver, BT on, LOGGER off:    62mA
3. Normal operation as a receiver, BT off, LOGGER on:    45mA
4. Normal operation as a receiver, BT disc*, LOGGER off: 48mA
5. Standby after timeout, BT off, LOGGER: off:            5mA

[*] disconnected

It takse about 10 seconds to wake up and obtain a proper fix,
so this standby mode *is* useful.
