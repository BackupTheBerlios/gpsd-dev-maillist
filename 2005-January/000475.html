One feature I really miss from the NMEA 0183 standard, is the ability
to report the pseudo range for the individual satellite and the other
information needed to do ones own position calculation based on the
raw data available to the GPS itself.

If this information was available in a standard format (it is
available in binary format from some GPSes), it would be easier to do
post-processing of the signal in an differencial GPS kind of way to
increase the accuracy of the detected positions.

Perhaps your document should mention the lack of a standard for
reporting this information?
