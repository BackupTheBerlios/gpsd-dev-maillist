> What are your plans regarding my restructuring of the NMEA parser

I have two reasons for hesitating.

First, I'm nervous about losing the recent progress with the GSV statement.

Second, your change implies adding new members to the gpsd_t structure.  I
don't want to add static storage to nmea_parse.c, as one of my design goals
is for both the service libraries to be reentrant.  This means any new 
persistent state has to become gpsd_t members.  This in turn means breaking
shared-library compatibility.
