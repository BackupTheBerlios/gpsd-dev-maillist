On Thu, 17 Jan 2008, Eric S. Raymond wrote:

> Currently our list of 'problem children' is Garn USB and the PPS support.

Garmin USB binary and Garmin Serial binary are working for me.  Patches,
as yet untested, for Garmin 5Hz PPS are in.  Fix for 'typename' is in.

Just one thing still bothering me.

This works for me with Garmin Serial binary and PPS:

	# /usr/local/sbin/gpsd -nN /dev/ttyS0

But this does not:

	# /usr/local/sbin/gpsd -n /dev/ttyS0

The latter goes into the background, but otherwise freezes and only a
'kill -9' will free it up.  If I compile with '--disable-pps' then I can
put gpsd in the background fine. ntpd time even works fine (w/o PPS of
course).  So the problem appears to be in backgrounding the PPS thread.

I'll beat on it some more but threads are not my thing and gpsd will not
log anything in background mode.

Ideas anyone?
