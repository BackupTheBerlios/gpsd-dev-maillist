Hi,
      I want to clarify data flow from GPS device till Application.
 
       My Understanding of Data Flow:
       1)Raw_Hook Callback will be  registered with Input Buffert o Serial 
Port Device driver  at the time of initialization of GPSd.
       2) Raw_Hook Callback will be   called whenever the GPS device sends 
some data on port.
       3)NMEA_parser will be called and position information will be 
extracted .
       4)Extracted position will be notified to Application via Socket 
Communiation.

    Please correct me if am wrong.
