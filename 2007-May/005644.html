On Thu, 17 May 2007, Eric S. Raymond wrote:

> >             1)How many processes are there in gpsd? what are those
> > processes?
>
> Just one.  gpsd is a single-threaded daemon built around an event-processing
> loop.

There is a special case that invokes a second process.  If your gps is
supplying gpsd with a PPS signal then a second process is spawned.  This
second process merely passes the PPS time stamp to the ntpd daemon.
