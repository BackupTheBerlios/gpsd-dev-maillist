I packaged some Perl modules over the holidays GPS::PRN and GPS::SpaceTrack and put them on CPAN.
 
 http://search.cpan.org/perldoc?GPS%3A%3ASpaceTrack
 http://search.cpan.org/perldoc?GPS%3A%3APRN

 See http://search.cpan.org/src/MRDVT/GPS-SpaceTrack-0.03/bin/example-plot.png
