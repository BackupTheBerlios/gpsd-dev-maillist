Looking at the RTCM decoder, it's trying to recognize the preamble at  
any bit shift. I wonder if this is really necessary. An RTCM word is  
30 bits which packes into exactly 5 bytes. If a word were allowed to  
be split such that one byte contained bits from two adjacent words  
you would have a situation where one packed could not be decoded  
until you started receiving the preamble for the next packed. If  
bytes can not be split across word boundaries it would make decoding  
much simpler.
