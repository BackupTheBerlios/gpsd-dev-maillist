There are actually a few streams outside Eurasia available on
www.igs-ip.net if you register at igs.bkg.bund.de.

AFAIK this service is open to people outside the EU as well.

On the IGS network there is at least:

DHLG3 in the USA at 33.39 244.21 providing a RTCM 2.3 stream

On the Misc network there are at least:

CALG0 int Canada at 51.11 245.96 providing a RTCM 2.1 stream
ERLA0 in the USA at 39.01 275.24 providing a RTCM 2.2 stream
OLAT0 in the USA at 38.91 265.22 providing a RTCM 2.1 stream
TAMP1 in the USA at 28.13 277.82 providing a RTCM 2.3 stream
THUN0 in the USA at 47.22 237.24 providing a RTCM 2.3 stream

The RTIGS network seems to be closed, unfortunately.
