there is actually one case where passing WAAS data over the internet is
a good idea. The WAAS and EGNOS satellites are AFAIK in geostationary
orbit around the equator which means they aren't always visible in urban
(or natural) canyons, especially the farther you get towards the poles.
With WAAS (possibly converted to RTCM) over Ntrip you can still receive
these corrections.
