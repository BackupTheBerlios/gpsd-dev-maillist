I debugged a problem with one of my bluetooth gps receives (a qstarz  
BT-Q810) to gpsd occasionally receiving multiple NEMA sentences when  
it does one "read". It doesn't seem to be able to parse multiple  
sentences from one read.

I see the buffer, which is normally used to just store sentence  
fragments, rises up to a large value.

I temporarily solved the problem by reducing the read buffer to 20  
bytes. That way I guarantee that I can't get multiple sentences.  
Instead I get lots of fragments.

Has anyone seen this before? Any opinions?
