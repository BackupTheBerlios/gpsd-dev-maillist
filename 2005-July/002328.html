Is your Hicom realy dead? Myaby it is only switched to binary mode. That
happend to me some time ago and that is why I started to study EverMore binary
protocol...

As I know, $PEMT string is sent by my EverMore GPS only after reset and after
that every 10 minutes. It is possible to issue binary command to do "reset".
Message 0x80. I am not sure if it is good idea to sent blidly this reset
"binary" command until it is sure that EverMore chip is connected. It could
have immpact on other GPSs devices.
