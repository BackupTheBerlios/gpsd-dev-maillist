$GPRMC is accepted when possition is valid. Here is example:

gpsd[12502]: gpsd: Character '*', new state: 3 
gpsd[12502]: gpsd: Character '6', new state: 3 
gpsd[12502]: gpsd: Character '7', new state: 3 
gpsd[12502]: gpsd: GPS sent 0 characters 
gpsd[12502]: gpsd: Character 0x0d, new state: 4 
gpsd[12502]: gpsd: Character 0x0a, new state: 5 
gpsd[12502]: gpsd: GPS sent 76 characters 
gpsd[12502]: gpsd: <= GPS:
$GPRMC,195127.000,A,5004.6993,N,01425.2434,E,000.0,007.9,241185,000.4,E*67^M 

When possition is not valid, gpsd reports "unknown sentence" in the log;
Haicom HI-203E GPS...
