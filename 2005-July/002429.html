Oleg Gusev <oleg at crista.uni-wuppertal.de>:
> Some more questions:
> 1. Should the $PFST replies handled in some way ?

I was wondering that myself.  You might want to consider replacing
literal_send with a function that does the send, then calls packet_get()
to grab the return and see if it's an ERR response or an echo.

> 2. How do i gracefully ask gpsd to up the speed to 115200 ?

See the B command.  That will change speed on gpsd's end.  It will
also call whatever iTrax-specific method you have for changing the 
device speed.

> 3. There is no way (according to the documentation) to switch back
>     to NMEA from ITALK binary in software. Ist it a problem for gpsd ?

It shouldn't be. The packet getter should recognize iTalk bainary
packets and switch to that driver automatically, without needing to go
through iTrax NMEA first.  

Of course I haven't been able to test the packet-getter on iTalk packets.
Is it working?
