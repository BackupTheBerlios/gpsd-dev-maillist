> Hello,
>
> EverMore GPS has message 0x08, it is Measurement Data message. It has
> information for every sattelite channel including Pseudo Range, Delta
> range,
> Doppler. One byte there is reserved in every channel.
>
> Could be this data with subframe information?
> Is packet 0x08 interested for us? It is almoust ignored now by EverMore
> driver.

Most receivers have this.  It is not very useful for us.  You could
calculate your own fix from information like this, or use it to validate
the calculations made in the receiver (e.g. as a developer or researcher).

Are you still looking for the UTC offset?  That is located in the 50bps
data (almanac/ephemeris).  Maybe your receiver can send it on request.
The almanac is a very complex piece of data, because details of the
modulation scheme are visible in the data returned from the receiver, and
you have to do many checks and handling yourself.  For example, the data
is sometimes inverted (I presume for spectral reasons, after modulation)
and this has to be undone.

The SiRF driver has code to extract the UTC offset from it but I never got
it working on my receiver.  Others have got data but found strange bugs in
the data from the receiver.
The Zodiac and Trimble receivers just return the offset value in some
field in one of the messages, that is of course much more convenient :-)
