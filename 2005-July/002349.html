I've committed a first cut at Evermore binary support.  Caveats:

1. This code is completely untested.  I wrote it today by looking at
   the Evermore protocol docs.

2. Parsing of the channel- and satellite-data packets is not
   completely implemented.  The part I left out is some 
   trivial but fussy bit-bashing.

3. The driver depends on the assumption that the "clock offset" in
   the Satellite Data Output packet is a leap-second offset (the
   manual has nothing to say about it besides the name).

4. There's no probe string.  This means the driver won't take
   over until the GPS emits $PEMT, which could be as long as
   10 minutes after startup.

Somebody with an Evermore should test and debug this.  It will
probably take about 4 hours' work.
