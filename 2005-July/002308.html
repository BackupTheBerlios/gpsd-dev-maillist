> shipping PGRME.  Presumably the increase in accuracy will
> already be folded into those figures.
> 
> Chris, how do we tell if a SiRF chip has WAAS lock?  I'd assume
> it was message 50 but the firlds in that don't look quite right.

You have a few options:
1) ignore the problem entirely and just wait for message 17 (DGPS corrections)
2) parse message 29 (NavLib DGPS Data)
3) message 2 (measured navigation data), field "mode1", bit7 is the
"DGPS Corrections Applied" flag
4) message 41(Geodetic Navigation Data), field "nav type", bit 7 is
the "DGPS Corrections Applied" flag
