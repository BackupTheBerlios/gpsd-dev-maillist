> Hm.  By "a feed", dou you mean a DGPSIP server making data from the
> whole CORS network available as RTCM records?

Yes, but as separate streams, one from each GPS.

I believe that CORS GPS's already generate the required RTCM-SC104
data stream on their serial ports.  Right now this data is just logged
to some computer that records the RTCM data in some standard
human-readable format.  What would be really nice is getting the CORS
folks to add a two-wire tap to the serial line (RX and Ground) and
send us the resulting bit-stream over the net.

That could put quite a large percentage of the country into areas
covered by very good differential corrections.
