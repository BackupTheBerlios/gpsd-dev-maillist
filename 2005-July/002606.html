lists I subscribed to.

NEVER been able to change it to SiRF mode or recognize it as a SiRF  
GPS.  It has worked OK in NMEA mode.  With 2.26 & 2.28, gpsd has

not get good data from gpsd.

6.  'cat.output' is the output of 'cat /dev/cu.gps > cat.output'.  If  
there's anything else I can provide/do to help troubleshoot this,  
please let me know.
