> That is not a useful value, it is a diagnostic for the manufacturer.
> 
> You need to look for something like "UTC offset" or "GMT offset" that has
> value 13 currently.  When the receiver sends this value it must be
> transferred to session->context->leap_seconds.
> Furthermore you need to find out if times reported by the receiver are in
> UTC or in GPS time.  When they are in GPS time the above value must be
> subtracted from all time values before storing them.
> 
> Seeing that this is already being done, it might well be that evermore
> reports all times in UTC, and you have to remove this subtraction.
> But, first find out if something is written in the documentation.  There
> may be some statusbit that indicates if times are UTC or GPS, and there
> may be some command to switch between them.
