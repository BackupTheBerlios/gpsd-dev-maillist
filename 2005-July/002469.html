I don't know how to do it from gcc but objdump can help.  This gets
you enough of the lines of code to find your place:

    gcc -O2 -g -gstabs rtcmdecode.c -o rtcmdecode
    objdump rtcmdecode -d -S > rtcmdecode.objdump
