At this point I think the support patch integration is finished.
Rather than change the user-visible interface, I re-coded gps_open()
to connect with AF_UNSPEC, AF_INET, or AF_INET6 depending on the
form of the server address.  This feature also makes client-side
-4 and -6 switches unnecessary.

The only remaining issue is what to do with configure.ac.  I'm tempted
to say "nothing", because the LOC and storage overhead of supporting
both protocol families rather than just one is so low. I think the
difference comes down to about ten LOC and a couple of 64-bit variables.

Under the circumstances, I'm not sure it's worth complicating the 
configuration system to be selective.  Do you have an opinion about this?
