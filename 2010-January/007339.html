Making a Thunderbolt-E work.

They seem to have changed from 8O1 to 8N1 in the version of TSIP that
Thunderbolt E uses.  I patched
  my local copy of driver_tsip.c to use 8N1, and it at least appears to
detect the TSIP box, and gets
  some data from it successfully.

The cgps app appears to get PRN data, but never any "fixes"--even after
the Trimble unit indicates
  that it's all initialized, and there are multiple (8 or more)
satellites in view.  I wonder how much
  the TSIP protocol has changed?

Strangely enough, even Trimbles *own* app fails to synch-up with the
newer Thunderbolt E boxes, since
  it insists on 8O1, and there's no way to change it to 8N1.
