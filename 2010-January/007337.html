Hi Gary,

thanks for the Christmas present in form of non-root ntpshm usage.  :-)
The included comments on how to debug shm segments were very useful.

The attached patch suggests a variation of the logics which removes the 
necessity for gpsd to be started before ntpd.  I believe it to follow the 
spirit of the shm permissions set up by ntpd -- see comments in the patch.

It also changes the permissions from 0700 and 0777 to 0600 and 0666 as is 
done in ntp-4.2.6.

I've tested starting both ntpd and gpsd first after removing the segments, 
as root and non-root.

Cheers,
Håkan
