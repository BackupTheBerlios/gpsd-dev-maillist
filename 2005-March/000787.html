On Thu, 3 Mar 2005, Greg KH wrote:

> You can poke around in /proc/tty/driver/usbserial to determine exactly
> what usb-serial device is attached to what ttyUSB0 node on 2.4.

# cat /proc/tty/driver/usbserial
usbserinfo:1.0 driver:v2.0
0: module:garmin_gps name:"Garmin GPS usb/tty" vendor:091e product:0003 num_ports:1 port:1 path:usb-0000:00:1d.0-1


Now that looks REAL easy to parse.  No symlinks to follow, etc.

If "garmin_gps" is not there then there is no point probing further.

Thanks.  Been way too long since I have mucked with drivers...
