I see you did all the work on gpsprof.  Looks good.

Just one comment.  It is now harder to use for the novice.  It seems
to me that a simple "gpsprof" on the command line should do something
useful, or at least pretty.  No need to expose the machinery to the
novice unless he asks for it.

on the terminal unless otherwise instructed.  If the user wants to
play w/ the data he can use the "-o [file]" to save the data.  Maybe
a special case of "-o -" to send the plotfile to stdout.

Your thoughts?
