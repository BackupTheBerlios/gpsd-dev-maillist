This actually works better than I would have expected.  Very acceptable

My laptop is usually within a few mS of stratum one time.

When I compare my stable ntp time to the gpsd time I get an offset of 10
to 50mS with a "dispersion" (jitter) of about 15mS.  The offset will be

I will have to adjust the ntp config just a bit after I get a hang
for how this is working.  With 4 good net time sources the local ntpd is
sometimes locking on the the gpsd time even though it is slightly off.

so it gets used only when it really is the best source.
