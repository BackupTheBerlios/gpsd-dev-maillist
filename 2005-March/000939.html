That's such a long time that I don't believe it.  Each baud hunt scans
600 characters.  4800 baud at 8N1 ships 533.3 chars/sec, so failing
should take about 1.1 seconds after which 9600 is the next one tried.
The longest sync time you should *ever* see is less than 2.2 seconds.
Either your UART is doing something ridiculous or there's something
else going on.
