Aaaaarrrrggggh.  My bad.  But I'm adding this to my list of gripes about 
the minual:

Table 2-7:

At this and other places where you list control bits, a reader not
paying close attention can mistake the bit numbers for bitmask
constants.  An easy way to head this off is to include both the bit
number and the hex mask, as follows:

   1 = 0x01
   2 = 0x02
   3 = 0x04
   4 = 0x08
     :
