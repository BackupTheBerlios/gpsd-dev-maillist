On Fri, 11 Mar 2005, Remco Treffkorn wrote:

> And DGPS is independent of SA. I don't know what WAAS and EGNOS policy is, in
> case the US turns on SA.

I don't think they'll turn SA back on again, but there is a page at raytheon
about WAAS, and they talk about intentional signal degradation:

 	WAAS includes a capability called "Military Emergency Mode".
 	In this mode, the WAAS signal is broadcast, but there are no
 	fast corrections. This mode is invoked at the prerogative of
 	the US Air Force. There have been no discussions that I am
 	aware of to date regarding using this mode in the near future.
 	In general, Raytheon views this as an unlikely possibility.

 	If we are requested to change to Military Emergency Mode, we
 	will update the WAAS Web page with an indication that the signal
 	is intentionally being degraded. If we can, we will also post to
 	the web page when we expect the system to be returned to Normal
 	mode.
