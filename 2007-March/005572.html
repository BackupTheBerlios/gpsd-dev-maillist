Vendors can change the usb description strings without changing
product id, I think.

You may be able to find out what driver claimed which device with the
following tools:
lsmod
kldstat
kextstat
modstat
dmesg

The following tools may help you discover what's on your usb bus
lsusb
usbdevs
dmesg
system_profiler
