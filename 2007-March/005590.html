We have a UBX document up... thought it sounds like you already have a copy.

> Next step: anyone want to point me in the right direction of how to send UBX
> packets into the device via GPSd?

have a look at sirfmon, and how it connects to the control socket to
send bytes. of course, you get to build your own packets.

Failing that, you could have a look at contrib/nmeasend.c and use that
as kind of a guide: take the packet payload on the command line (maybe
in hex, if it's a binary protocol), checksum it and add the
appropriate encapsulation.
