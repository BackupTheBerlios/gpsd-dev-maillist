Hi

I am interested in making some enhancements to gpsd so it's more useful
in an embedded device (such as a gumstix board):

* Drop the gpsd->gps connection when no clients are connected to gpsd.

* Open and close the DGPS connection on demand.

* Repeat corrected NMEA data over bluetooth rfcomm.

With these mods, the embedded board could either make a bluetooth gps or
its own gps module available with corrections to multiple bluetooth
clients. It would also be robust enough to deal with bluetooth
connections coming and going and conserving power.

Has anyone worked on any features like this?
