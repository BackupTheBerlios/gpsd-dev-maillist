It's truly annoying to have to do this just after crowing over our
good Coverity results from March, but the project is in a bit of a
hole right now because persons who should know better got careless
about the regression tests since then.

And yes, that includes me; if I had been running regressions as often as I
should have, I'd have caught the problems sooner and we wouldn't have as
nasty a mess on our hands.

All gpsd committers should read the new section in the Hacking Guide titled
"Verify your patch or commit".  It's at 

	<http://gpsd.berlios.de/hacking.html#verify>. 

This is project policy. It's what we have to do to maintain a defect
rate of less than 1 per 10K LOC.  Take it seriously, and respect your
co-developers by not causing unnecessary problems for them to have
to clean up.
