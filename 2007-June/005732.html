No formal testing protocol. We'd love it if we had a test lab where we
could specifically qualify  various configurations of gpsd (svn plus
the last two releases) on various processors (arm, amd64, i386, hppa,
sparc64, ppc) with various os-es (linux, solaris, *BSD, AIX, HP/UX). I
was thinking about that this morning, and that would cost a lot of
money.

So we do the next best thing: we ask our users to tell us a "works for
me" story. Just let us know which version of gpsd you're running, what
platform, what kind of receiver (maybe include the firmware version if
it's easy to get) and whether or not you had to do anything special.
The general idea is for someone to do as you did - look on the
hardware list and say "oh, i can get one of those!"

CK
