If you need a stable GPS-18 right now then compile w/ --disable-garmin

Toi turn off garmin binary send this to the unit:
	"\x10\x0A\x02\x26\x00\xCE\x10\x03"
	$PGRMC1,1,1,2,,,2,W,N,1,1,2
        $PGRMI,,,,,,,R

Not sure what the checksum should be.  I guess I should have a utility
like sirfmon to do this.
