I've decided to try my hand at finding out when nmea broke. Test pipeline

nmea -> perl-> pty/tty -> [1] gpsd[2] -> cgps

[1] I have month of nmea logged in a file. I feed it to gpsd via a pty
with a perl script.

[2] cgps reads gpsd-protocol from the version under test. i patched
the nmea parser to ignore pgrme entirely, because until very recently
the output format and parser were wrong.

Results so far:

r3516: broken
r3400: works
r3450: broken: cycles between no fix, 2d, 3d
r3425: broken: ""
r3413: broken: ""
r3406: works
r3410: broken
r3408: broken
r3407: works

so... whatever broke, it broke between 3407 and 3408...
