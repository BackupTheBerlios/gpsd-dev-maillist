What your change does is remove the check for valid fix.  This means PPS
pulses are accepted (and used by ntp) even when there is no fix.
Many receivers output PPS pulses even when they have no lock at all with
satellites.  So you are just locking onto invalid data.
Also note you need a 3D fix for confidence in time, but people only
wanting wristwacht timesync may be willing accept a 2D fix.
No fix at all, receiving only one or two satellites, may result in time
several hundreds of milliseconds off.

>> Gary, on the other hand, has concluded that the check should be fixed
