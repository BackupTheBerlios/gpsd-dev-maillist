I know a lot about IPv6, at least on paper, and have discussed its
fate with many nanog and IETF members.  IPv6, by design, is in my mind
fatally flawed.  I could go on at length over why IPv6 has been a total
failure so far, and why IMHO it will be a failure in the future, but
this is not the forum for that.

problems.  Yes I have tried 6to4 and have had a public IPv6 net from a

None of this is relevant to gpsd.  If you want to give this a shot, then

> Living in Finland (about 60 degrees North), I can honestly say

Well have at it.  As Eric said, there is some RTCM, DGPSIP, and Ntrip
