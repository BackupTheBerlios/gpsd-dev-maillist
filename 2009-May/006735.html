> fail to see why that makes sense... Merkaartor has nothing but a minimal
> support for NMEA and a bit of support for gpsd and I can't see why one

> gpsd support in Merkaartor.
>
>
> Bernd

Gpsd was written, so applications could get all the location information from 
it using a standardized api, hiding all the details about manufacturer 
specific protocols. If Merkaartor would only support gpsd, it would not have 
to worry about Garmin, Jupiter, NMEA ...

Translating from NMEA to gpsd to Garmin binary, or from Jupiter to gpsd to 
Garmin sounds silly. The common denominator should be gpsd. Gpsd was not meant 
to be a translator from any protocol X to any protocol Y. It could do that, 
but that way insanity lies...

It just seems more rational to teach apps gpsd. But nowadays that's a week 
argument :-(
