The attached file patches two problems that I've noticed with the binary
control part of commands through the control socket.

1) Errors in the parsing of the hex from the client are not reported.

2) An incorrect argument is used for the final length to be sent to the
GPS.

This has been somewhat tested with a Skytraq Venus GPS.
