Gary E. Miller <gem at rellim.com>:
> > Looks good.  I guess this means it's time to remove the "subframes" flag.
> 
> You mean the flag from the client to gpsd to enable subframe data
> transmission?  I can't imagine normal clients wanting to see that data.
> 
> I would like to see a flag given the gpsd to see ONLY the raw measurement
> data (subframes, raw measurements, etc).

The trouble with this is that it it opens a door we probably want to keep shut.

Up to now, all the WATCH flags except enable itself have been about 
presentation style, not content.  There were none that actually subset or
filtered the data stream coming off the device. 

Once we start adding filtering flags in the daeonon, it's hard to know where 
to stop.  Rather than field 2**N questions of the form "Why isn't there a
filter flag for the subset of data I'm interested in?" I think the right
answer is "That's the client's job."
