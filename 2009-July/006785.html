Attached is a monitor for the Ea, Bb and En (time raim) messages for the 
oncore binary format.  I've split the Ea message into one horisantal 
window for the position information and a usual vertical one for the SV 
info.  As the Bb message gives further SV info (az/el), and also for some 
more satellites, I placed that next to the Ea message, and rearrange the 
Bb entries to keep the SV with information in both windows at the same 
line as in the most recent Ea message.

Cheers,
Håkan
