Heya,

as mentioned in
https://developer.berlios.de/bugs/?func=detailbug&group_id=2116&bug_id=13467
the use of /proc/bus/usb is deprecated, and the /proc/bus directory will
be removed on more and more distributions. So please fix the driver to
use libusb + /dev/bus/usb instead, or stick with the sysfs (where the
proper way is libsysfs instead of reading files from /sys).

Thanks & cheers,
