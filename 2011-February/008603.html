> does the Garming USB GPS show up as is_serial...

Yes, it does.  is_serial is actually a flag meaning "this is a local
sensor"; it's otherwise only used to control whether a DEVICE response
includes speed/parity/stopbit members.

I'm actually not happy about the information overlap between is_serial
and the new service_type member.  I may try to abolish the former in
favor of the latter.
