During some routine research this morning I discovered that Fritz Ganter
started shipping pre-releases of gpsdrive-2.10 recently, after a hiatus that 
stretched back to 2004.  And that he's kept a promise he made to us in 2005 to 
drop the fork of gpsd-1 that gpsdrive had been carrying around.

This means the last fork of gpsd-1 that had any life left in it is
kaput.  And various remnant forks of old gpsd-1 versions were the
only real competition this project ever had.

We've outlasted, out-designed, and out-coded everybody else in the
open-source GPS space.  We are *it*.  Three muted cheers -- and a 
determination to stick to the high standards and good practices 
that got us here -- are in order.
