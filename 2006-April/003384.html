> Seems to me that gpsd used to do that and Eric removed that code.

I know that Eric's opinion is that gpsd should not provide the "buffering"
functionality, and that the clients should implement it themselves.
It has been discussed before.  I'm afraid I do not agree with it.

The effect of not having the buffering varies between drivers, and the
SiRF binary driver that Eric normally uses has relatively little problem
because a lot of data is returned in one single message.
Other drivers, like Trimble TSIP and NMEA are affected a lot more.

Any new solution that we come up with, should at least work with all
drivers and hopefully (more difficult to verify) with all different NMEA
receivers.
