The sentence types sent per cycle are not constant.
Some types repeat every second, others every five, or so.

IIRC, my solution was to age values individually or in groups.

Lets say your cycle length is one second, then you would timeout position and 
altitude after i.e. three seconds. If the gps failed, you would get at most 2 
seconds worth of stale data. Seems safe.

Of course, you could get a position almost a half a second old, with an 
altitude of almost 1.5 seconds. I thought that acceptable.

I have no idea how it's currently done.
