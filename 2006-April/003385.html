> gpsd-server

This seems like a lot of different packages for such a small project...
I agree that a part that depends on something huge like X should be
separate, so that gpsd can be installed without pulling in X.
Development support (.h files and linkable libraries) is also often kept
separate, but as gpsd is relatively small this may not be required.
IMHO, server, shared libraries, and commandline stuff that does not depend
on large packages like python can just as well be all in one package. 
Perl is usually mandatory anyway.
