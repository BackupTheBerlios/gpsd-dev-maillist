It's looking more and more (to me) like this really _is_ an OpenMotif version 
issue. I cannot figure out why Fedora Core would ship what is clearly 
described as a beta version.

It seems that either OpenMotif 2.3.0-beta is broken or it changed something 
that brings out something broken in the use of the Motif "List" widget in 
'xgps'. I browsed the Release Notes for OpenMotif 2.3.0 and didn't see 
anything obvious.

However, I do see 2 bug reports for OpenMotif 2.3.0-beta, one of which is 
marked as a duplicate of the other (despite the fact that the descriptions 
and authors differ):

   http://bugs.motifzone.net/long_list.cgi?buglist=1330

   http://bugs.motifzone.net/long_list.cgi?buglist=1331

This problem seems very closely related to the 'xgps' issue. Fortunately, the 
bug is marked 'FIXED', so I suspect the next release of OpenMotif 
(2.3.0-beta3?) will solve it.

Until then, unless I see something to change my mind, I suspect you (and all 
Fedora Core 5 users) will need to revert to an older OpenMotif release (e.g. 
2.2.2 like me, or even 2.2.3 or 2.2.4) to fix this.

If I find anything more (I want to try an experiment or two with the 'xgps' 
code), I'll certainly let you and the list know, but this looks pretty "cut 
'n' dried" to me -- bug in OpenMotif 2.3.0-beta.

Holler if you have any questions.
