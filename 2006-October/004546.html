You're blind. ;^)

But I sympathize. It took me a while to find it too on the Mailmain v2.1.8 
list I admin.

Go to 'Privacy options...', 'Sender filters'. In the text description, it 
should say this (with embedded links):

Non-member postings can be automatically accepted, held for moderation, 
rejected (bounced), or discarded, either individually or as a group. Any 
posting from a non-member who is not explicitly accepted, rejected, or 
discarded, will have their posting filtered by the general non-member rules.

Click the "discarded" link and it should give you this option:

Action to take for postings from non-members for which no explicit action is 
defined.

From there, you can just select "Discard" and "Submit Your Changes".

HTH. Holler if you have any problems....
