Using libgpsmm.h/gps.h I would desire a way to get also raw data from
gpsd and doing the only converting by the client software. That would
avoid multiple converting.

The TNTC provides following data types:
Angles in degrees (0.0 to 359.9°), mils (0 to 6399), milliradians (0 to
6282), or 16-bit integer (0 – 65535)

Choosing 16-bit I get a far better data accuracy (more accurate then the
sensor is).
