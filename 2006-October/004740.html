Or sometimes the receiver is just too confused - not gpsd. There is a
reason why most of the vendor tools spam the receivers with every
moderately relevant configuration message rather than trying to
detect: because the polite way just isn't reliable.

That reminds me... I think we should consider firing breaks and
extended breaks at devices if they're unresponsive. Some chipsets
and/or firmwares are sensitive to this and will reset the UART and IO
task... at least early SiRFstars do this, and I wouldn't be surprised
if other vendors had similar functionality.
