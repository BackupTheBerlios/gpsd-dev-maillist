On Tue, 24 Oct 2006, Petr Slansky wrote:

> do you have idea how accurate is time reported by GPS receiver when it has no

I can only speak to the Garmin.  They make no claims to accuracy until
four 3D fixes in a row have been reported.  Then they claim +/-  1uS
on the PPS line.

Garmin makes no claims at all on the accuracy of NMEA time, but
experience shows there would be an offset of a few 100mS and a jitter of
several more 100mS.
