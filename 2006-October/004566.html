On 9/25/06, Chris Kuethe <chris.kuethe at gmail.com> wrote:
> GPSD tries reconfigure a gps to the most useful protocol - usually
> that's the vendor binary. Alas this reconfiguration can be somewhat
> problematic with bluetooth receivers which is too bad, because most of
> the vendor protocols can transmit a complete navigation solution in
> one message.

As I almost Learned The Hard Way(tm), some OEMs cripple/break/remove
the vendor binary protocol. Like Wintec did with the WBT-200. I could
boost the UART speed to 115200 no problem, but the instant I kicked it
over to iTalk mode, I got no more output. Good thing a) the battery
was easily removable and b) Fastrax puts full-featured firmware
updates on their website.
