   It assumes generic NMEA when it's unable to get any usable reply to
the 'I' command for ten seconds, which makes sense when replaying a
prerecorded data set.  That seemed the safest default, I could also
have it exit, instead, but I figured plowing onward with the most
common receiver would make the most people happy.

   Like I said, the code needs a *ton* of cleanup, this is just to
prove that the idea works.  I'll work on cleanup as free time (what's
that?)  permits.  I also want to do some clever things with the data
fields to support smaller screen sizes, but one thing at a time...
