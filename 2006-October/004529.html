by tab indent i meant that each indent level is a tab (which my
terminal renders 8 spaces wide), and long lines are indented an
additional 4 spaces..

 	if (this || that || something_else || other_condition ||
	    magical_undocumented_thing)
		do_stuff();

1 tab in front of "if"
1 tab and 4 spaces in front of "magical"
2 tabs in front of "do_stuff"

so we have no preferred style... *sigh* while i try and stick with
openbsd's KNF for my own stuff, as long as we pick something that i
can apply on the fly with vim or use indent(1) to validate after the
fact, i'm not too picky.
