Gary E. Miller <gem at rellim.com>:
> I would like to see the fancy calcs so we can get super resolution from
> our GPS, but I agree it does not belong in gpsd.  I would chop the
> baby in half: have gpsd optionally pass on the sat phase and such to a
> client.  The client could then calc as it sees fit.

Agreed.  

I'm quite willing to have the nav data parer be part of this project; I
just don't want the gpsd core code to rely on it.  For most users the
kind of "fancy calcs" you're talking about would be *way* overkill, so
let's not make the victims of the bugs that relying on such an
additional layer would introduce.

I know it may be difficult to believe after the packet sniffer and
the multichannel support and all the other stuff I've added, but I ereally
do believe in keeing gpsd small and simple.
