Yo Eric!

On Mon, 30 Oct 2006, Eric S. Raymond wrote:

> I've actually been thinking recently that it would be nice to merge
> xgps and cgps into a single test client that chooses its display method
> depending on whether or not it's running under X.

There are people that understand gpsd, people that understand curses and
people that understand X widgets.  A much smaller group know 2 of them and
a really small group knows all three.  You really cut the number of
potential maintainers drastically when you merge them.  Certainly peices
of code should be shared, but not all of it.

I also hate to burden a small tool like cgps with the X libs.  X libs
are much less stable and less portable than the basic C libs I could no
longer move a cgps compliled on an X host to a host without X unless I
did a special build.  THis all adds complexity that will confound the
casual user and make more work for the regular user
