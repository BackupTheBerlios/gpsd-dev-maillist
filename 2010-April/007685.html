w13738 <w13738 at gmail.com>:
> I am trying to decide the right architecture to integrate my GPS
> device driver with GPSD. What is the reason the DBus support is remove
> from v3.0?

That decision is not yet final, but it looks as though gpsd + geoclue 
is a better combination than having gpsd do D-BUS itself.  

The geoclue people love DBUS and have it as a primary mission.  For us,
our DBUS code is a dusty backwater.  So I think we're going to recommend 
that people who want DBUS notifications from GPSD should install geoclue
as well and let it collect fixes from us via our socket interface, then
ship those to DBUS.

Is there any reason that wouldn't be a satisfactory solution for you?
