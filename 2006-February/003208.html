OK, thanks, Eric.

Actually, to be precise, what you did was not exactly what is stated above. 
You (correctly) did the alternate part of my suggestion:

Marr <marr at flex.com>:
> On the other hand, if there's a good reason for 14 as the
> maximum, 'xgps.h' [sic -- s/b 'xgps.c'] should eliminate the 
> 'MAX_CHANNELS' version and adjust 
> its code accordingly, thereby allowing display of up to 14 satellites in
> the Motif 'list' widget.

That is, you went with 14 as the new maximum overall number of satellites, 
which actually is the best choice, given all the useful comments on the issue 
(WAAS, etc).

However, in making the 'xgps' app display data for up to 14 satellites, you've 
inadvertently cut off the bottom widget (the line where all the incoming GPS 
data is displayed). 

To fix this, please also change this line in 'xgps.c':

   #define ROOT_SIZE	"700x570"	/* size of entire xgps display */

to use "700x606" instead, to maintain the bottom line.

Aside: This 'xgps' app is badly written in general (hard-coding window 
size?!?) and could use a modernization at some point.
