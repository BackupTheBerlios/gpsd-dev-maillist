  project repository to Mercurial or git.  A couple of things to note:

I have been wrestling with the same question about BBN's internal source
control, except with bzr in the mix.  Various people have played with
various ones.  In particular, our linux kernel wizard tried git and ran
screaming that it was hard to understand and to deal with - and this is
the guy that figures out everything that stumps the rest.  Two other

git.
