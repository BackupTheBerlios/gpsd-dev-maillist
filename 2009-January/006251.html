I don't think so.

You can verify it by replacing gps_query(collect,buf) with
gps_unpack(strdup(buf), collect) in libgps.c and running libgps to
simulate a gpsd message like this:
gpsd-2.38rc1> ./libgps "$(echo -e 'GPSD,O=RMC 1207318966.000 0.005
49.026225 12.188348 375.20 19.20 10.40 70.8900 24.899 0.000 75.6699
38.40 ? 3\n$GPVTG,70.89,T,,M,48.40,N,89.6,K,A*34')"
