Not yet, but I'd love to see such a thing. In a way, we have part of it
already: "R=2" mode with certain smart devices, like ublox, itrax and sirf.

I've been trying to think of how to add a raw data midlayer to gpsd - to my
mind it'd be much like our other packet decoders but the destination struct
and the unpacking would be a bit messier. Long term goal of that would be to
present all raw data for use by software solvers like gpstk.
