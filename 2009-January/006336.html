I think the form at:

  https://www.mainframe.cx/cgi-bin/gps_report.cgi

should be a bit more clear about privacy issues, perhaps by adding a new
section at the beginning, explaining that the output sample (which will
contain location) and all other information will be published.
Currently it says:

  Contact information

  Important! We need a valid email address for you in case we need to ask
  you followup questions about the device. We won't give your address to
  anyone.  Example: Eric Raymond <esr at thyrsus.com>


(I am not objecting to what's being done at all, just asking that people
be properly warned.)


perhaps just add at the top.


Privacy of Behavior Report

Everything in your report will be posted on the developers' list and
thus become public and appear in various mailinglist archives.  This
includes your email address and the sample data, which almost certainly
contains location information for where you conducted the test.
