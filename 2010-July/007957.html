  Hello,

  bringing daemon support to gpxlogger, I've noticed that
gpsd itself doesn't utilize the daemon(3) function that
is available on all BSDs and on most Linuxes.

I fingers refused to make cut-n-paste of the daemonize()
function from gpsd.c to gpxlogger.c, which itself is cut-n-paste
from a libc on most platforms :)

So here is a patch that pushes daemonize() to a separate
file, that can be linked both to gpsd and to gpxlogger.
But on most systems this file won't be compiled at all,
a library functions would be utilized instead.
