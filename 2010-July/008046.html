Bernd,
make dist-rpm fails due to the .la files.
 
somehow this rm command got commented out in the gpsd.spec.in file.
 
diff --git a/packaging/rpm/gpsd.spec.in b/packaging/rpm/gpsd.spec.in
-#rm -f %{buildroot}%{_libdir}/libgps*.la
+rm -f %{buildroot}%{_libdir}/libgps*.la

The other option is to not install the *.la files in "make install".

Otherwise it works great.
