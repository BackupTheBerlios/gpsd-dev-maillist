Yo Jeff!

On Sun, 25 Sep 2005, Jeff Francis wrote:

>   Before I attempt a patch, I want to verify my sanity.  My
> understanding is that the NMEA-0183 standard says that the GPRMC
> sentence reports speed in knots.

That would make sense.  After all NMEA is a marine standard.

> and uses the 'session->gpsdata.fix.speed' for the speed field.
> According to gps.h line 65, that field is Meters/second, not knots.

Yes, fix.speed is meters/second.  I am the one that patched in a lot of
the units in the comments.  All the drivers, not just the NMEA driver,
write to the fix.speed field.  If the NMEA driver gets it wrong then
just fix the NMEA driver.  If you change the meaning of that field then
you have to change all the drivers that currently get it right.
