I didn't get a very good feeling about trying to contact globalsat
support online.  They have a few pages of small type that you need to
agree to before they even let you send a web-based msg to support.
(All that just to get an RMA where you get to pay shipping both ways
plus pay a handling fee to them.)

I agree with Remco, my bu-353 w. orignal firmware has a glaring bug
that makes it pretty much useless for me.  The heading data in msg-41
(the lat/lon based position message) indicates a constant heading to
0.0 degrees whenever one is heading in a northerly direction.  Driving
south the numbers appear to be correct.  (Perhaps this is related to
nav-lock, where someone mistakenly pinned the heading to zero whenever
the number was less than some cutoff?)

Has anyone taken their bu-353 on a test drive and noticed something
similar?  I didn't get a chance to test the xyz-based navigation msg
to see if it had similar problems.
