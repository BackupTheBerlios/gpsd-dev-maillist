Right now there are two different issues:

1. sometimes values are not correctly remembered and a ? is put in a place
where one message ago a valid value was shown.

2. the O output is repeated for every message received from the receiver,
instead of only once per cycle.

What is your opinion about this?  Should we receive 4 O lines per second
when the receiver happens to send its info in 4 separate messages?  Or
should they be accumulated in a single message per second even when this
sometimes delays info from the receiver a bit?
