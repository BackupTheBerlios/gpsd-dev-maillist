I've removed CBAUDEX from serial.c on account of it not existing everywhere.

http://www.mcsr.olemiss.edu/cgi-bin/man-cgi?termios+3
Says that CBAUD and CBAUDEX are non-posix extensions.

http://www.openbsd.org/cgi-bin/man.cgi?query=termios&manpath=OpenBSD+Current&format=html
says that openbsd doesn't have these.

http://www.freebsd.org/cgi/man.cgi?query=termios&apropos=0&sektion=0&manpath=FreeBSD+5.3-RELEASE+and+Ports&format=html
freebsd doesn't, either.

http://www.freebsd.org/cgi/man.cgi?query=termios&apropos=0&sektion=0&manpath=NetBSD+2.0&format=html
nor does netbsd.
