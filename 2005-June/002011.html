Gary E. Miller <gem at rellim.com>:
> I can understand how it affects people badly when the core parts of
> gpsd are broken, but the add ons rarely do that and add significant
> value to the project.

I agree.  Furthermore, please note that I designed the new
strategy-pattern architecture specifically to decouple the 
add-ons from the library core and the library core from the
daemon logic.  

I believe our experience shows this redesign was successful.
The bugs we have been experiencing are *not* the results of 
clashing assumptions in the add-ons or design problems in the
way the add-ons are mated to the core.

Therefore I believe we don't nreed to be nervous about the
existing add-ons, or even about new add-ons.
