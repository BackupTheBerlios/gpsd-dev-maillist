It's a complete failure in my environment -- all the "?" reports in
the O response come out "nan", instead.  I suspect this has to do with
equality comparisons on NAN values not working.

The regression-test suite is there to catch things like this.  Please 
test your next attempt with it.

in that case your hack of setting NAN to 1e38 if the compilation environment
doesn't supply it won't work.
