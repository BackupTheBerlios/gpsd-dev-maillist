Well, there seem to be several.  And now I read on this list that they
even exist with 20 channels.

This only confirms my suspicion that it has more to do with feature lists
than with actual usability.  The longest vis list I have ever seen had 14
sats.  So 12 channels may sometimes not be enough, and 16 allows for WAAS
and some expansion when extra sats would be launched.

But 20??  Even for cold-start acquisition speed the advantage starts to
diminish at that count...

Really useful 20 channel or more receivers should be able to receive
Glonass and Galileo.
