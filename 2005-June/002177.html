> TSIP still has problems, I have not yet found why.

It is because eph and epv are part of the fix structure.
So, when CYCLE_START_SET is set the values of eph and epv are cleared and
they are only recalculated when a packet containing DOP comes in.  This
does not happen every cycle but only every 5 seconds.  So most of the time
the eph and epv are shown as n/a.

In the meantime I have many other problems with this receiver.
When I telnet and type R the driver segfaults on sending the NMEA string.

While initializing it has trouble getting the correct baudrate.  This has
always been difficult (because the receiver sends little data when idling)
but now it has become difficult to get it synced.
Maybe it is caused by my changes, I have to check again.
