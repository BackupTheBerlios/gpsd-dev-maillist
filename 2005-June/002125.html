I think the whole concept of ellipsoid separation has been introduced in
gpsd not that long ago.
As it is now, it looks like all other drivers use MSL height for the
altitude field, and separation in a different variable to convert to WGS
when required.  When looking at the receiver protocols it seems that (with
the exception of NMEA) the general consensus is to output height above WGS
ellipsoid, and leave the conversion to MSL to the user.
