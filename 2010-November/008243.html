These patches for oncore pps behaviour are based on my previous patch 
today.

- Allow oncore devices in postion hold mode to deliver PPS data to ntpshm.

- Take programmed oncore PPS offset into account.
   Name correction delay -> offset.

- Do not modify TRAIM on startup, instead poll it.
   Sawtooth-correction from TRAIM message is parsed, but not used.

Please consider for inclusion.

Cheers,
