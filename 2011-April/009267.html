>> 1. NO_FLOAT

I'll try some benchmarks, but there tends to be problems the faster
the data goes (an occasional 1% at 1Hz can turn into several percent
at 20Hz).

It also gets more important the lower you go.  The 200Mhz and under
MIPS (think DD-WRT router) without floating point would be even worse.
 My fully optimized integer minigpsd was using about a half-percent
CPU per Hz.

At the moment I'm working directly with the Venus 638 firmware since
it is a 50MHz sparc core.  I think I could get json out of the second
serial port :).
