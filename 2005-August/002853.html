Hi everybody,

What do you think about extending the info available from gpsd via dbus?
Currently it seems to be limited to the out put of the 'o' command. As far
as I know the only app that currently makes use of dbus is gpxlogger, which
should log dop values (allows post-processing apps to remove points with
high dop). Also some other apps could be ported (xgps, gpsdrive) to dbus
that make use of dop and satellite data.
I haven't much looked into dbus and therefore know nothing about overhead,
perfomance or memory issues, but my proposal would be enhancing the dbus
info in this way:
a) Add dop values to org.gpsd.fix
b) New signal org.gpsd.sat. This transports the following members of
gps_data_t: satellites_used, used, satellites, elevation, azimuth, ss, maybe
PRN (I have no idea what this is =) ). Maybe add a timecode, although I
don't think this is critical for these data.
