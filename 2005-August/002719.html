> I am getting something funny.  Running the serial port at 4800N1, yet
> there are parity errors in the logs!
>
> gpsd: parity failure, lost lock
>
> WTF?

Parity is something that can be used at the link level (and which you
don't use, the 4800N1 setting), or it can be used at the message level.
In this case, IS-GPS-200 almanac and RTCM data has parity in its messages
and the error message is about that.
