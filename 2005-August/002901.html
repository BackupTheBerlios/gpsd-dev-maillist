> 'undo' was a bad choice of word. What I really meant by it is if gpsd

You still don't get it.  In general, gpsd cannot know if it switched
something off.  All it knows is whether it sent the switchoff sequence;
it doesn't know whether the switch was on before, so it can't know
what the right value to reset to is.
