Our patches just crossed.  I just checked in a patch to add the -l
option to cgps.  Here is how it works:
	-l d     - Show DD.dddddd
	-l m     - Show DD MM.mmmm'
        -l s     - Show DD MM' SS.sss"

DD.dddddd is the default.

my math.  Yesterday's patch was off in the .sss part by 10.
