I'm fairly impressed with the weather resistance of these two
receivers. I've had them sitting outside on the roof of a building for
a couple of weeks now, connected to a soekris in a pelican case and
they've been working very well, no extra silicone required. It's been
fairly wet over the last week and chilly too - getting as low as
41F/5C, with moisture ranging from heavy mist through drizzle right up
to a proper rainstorm. No sign of errors, unlike the time I left a
receiver outside overnight and the lawn sprinkler system shorted out
the connector on my USB extension cable...
