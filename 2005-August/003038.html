I applied most of the cgps patches.  Looks like you fixed several bugs I
added yesterday.  The RTCM does NOT need the BINARY_ENABLE code as
it works just fine (or should) in NMEA only mode.

I did NOT apply the garmin patch.  The problem is that there are two
"garmins".  One is the garmin binary driver and the other is the garmin
NMEA driver.  The garmin NMEA driver is just an extra initializer
step to optimize the serial data, so you would not want that in the
GARMIN_ENABLE.
