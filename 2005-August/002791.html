> detects a gps at 57600, and then on re-open it goes to 9600 and stays

I observe the same problem. It is easy repeatable. My GPS is conected to
serial port ttyS0; My Linux kernel is 2.4.18mdk.

1) set your GPS to baudrate different from 9600; like 4800 or 19200
2) start gpsd
3) connect with telnet to gpsd
4) use I, B and R command to check that GPS was recognised and speeed is
correct
5) close telnet connection
6) connect with telnet to gpsd
7) use B and R command to see that speed is 9600 and no data comes from GPS
8) check log files...

I think that problem is in gpsd_close() call or gpsd_open() call that restores
wrong parameters to serial port.
