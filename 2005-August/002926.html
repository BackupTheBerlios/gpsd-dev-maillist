> Has anyone else used the Navman jupiter pico (

I coldstarted my GPS-18/LVC while covered with a steel plate.  Then
started gpsd, no problems.  Then I removed the plate and allowed it to
get a fix, no problems.

The regression for the GPS-18/LVC has a similar sequence of data in it.

OTOH, it always sent NMEA on the sats it expected to be seeing, so the
GPGSA, GPGSV were never empty.
