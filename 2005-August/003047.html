Yo Eric!

Not so fast.  The new option is a /dev/tty*, the -p is for a psuedo device.

The idea of -p is to create a device that another local program can open
like a serial device.  That way an NMEA aware program that is not gpsd
aware can have an NMEA feed sent to it over a fake serial device.

The -s option goes out a real serial port to an external device.
