Name: GP-27 (http://www.cpit.com/en/GP-27.html)
Compatibility: Excellent
Chipset: Nemerix
Interface: Bluetooth
Vendor: Central Pacific (http://www.cpit.com)
Works with: 2.29-pre (older versions also work, but report errors on RMC and
VTG)
NMEA: 3.01
Sentences:
GPGGA,GPGLL,GPGSA,GPRMC,GPZDA,GPVTG,PNMRX300*,PNMRX301*,PNMRX302*,PNMRX303*,PNMRX304*,PNMRX603*
Notes:
- The maked (*) sentences are only sent on change or by request (603 only by
request)
- Several sencences can be sent to the device to change settings or request
information. DO NOT USE THE PNMRX100 SENTENCE TO CHANGE THE BAUD RATE! This
is not supported by the bluetooth chip on the device.
- Settings are saved in flash powered by a backup battery and persistant
over connections and when you turn it off.
Reported by Tobias Minich (belgabor at gmx.de)
