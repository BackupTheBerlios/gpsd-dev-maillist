I think it is good that gpsd saves the serial port status and restores it
when exiting without finding anything, but when it has actually detected a
receiver at a certain bitrate wouldn't it be better to leave the port at
that setting?

That way, gpsd will also start faster next time, because it always tries
the current setting first.  As it is now, it goes back to the setting made
before gpsd first started, which is usually the default of 9600 and that
is not often correct for a GPS receiver...
