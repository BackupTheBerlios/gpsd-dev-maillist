Several satellites with GPS receivers have already been launched.
Even radio amateurs have put GPS receivers in space.
It seems that the "Trimble Tans" receiver is popular.

A special receiver is required because speeds at which satellites move
causes higher doppler shift than a GPS receiver would normally be designed
to handle.

For more information, see http://centaur.sstl.co.uk/SSHP/list/list_gps.html
