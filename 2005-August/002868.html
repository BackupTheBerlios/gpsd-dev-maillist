administered by a master, and fought with distinction in an honor
tournament and a field battle, I'm now a qualified fighter in the
Southern Italian style of cut-and-thrust fencing.  For those of
you interested in such details, this is a shield-and-sword or 
single-sword battlefield style using a hand-and-a-half sword a bit 
lighter and longer than a true medieval broadsword and with a
thrusting point.
