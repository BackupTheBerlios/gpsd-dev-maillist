Rob Janssen <gpsd at xs4all.nl>:
> General remark: we should be very careful to avoid cases of fast spinning
> loops in gpsd.  As we are running at elevated priority, such fast spinning
> can be deadly.

I agree.  There is a Scylla and Charybdis here: on the one hand, sleeps
are bad because they stall all channels, on the other fast spins at
elevated priority are an effective way to eat CPU to no purpose.
