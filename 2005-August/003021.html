  Maybe.  The idea of running the C program is that it sets all of
your flow control, etc for the port appropriately.  For example,
serial ports generally default to 9600 baud, and the "official" NMEA
speed is 4800 baud.  Will 9600 baud NMEA work?  Sure, sometimes for
some things.  Your end device may or may not need re-configuring for a
higher speed.  Again, it's something I use, if you find it useful as
well, please feel free to use it.  If not, that's fine, too.
