Fellow 'gpsd' devs,

I noticed that the help output for 'gpsprof -h' shows two options ('-r' and 
'-o file') which are not supported by that application/script at all.

As such, I've deleted those (obsolete? never-supported?) options from the 
description in the help output and checked that change into SVN.

I don't think they were ever supported or documented. I looked back to 
gpsd-2.25 (the oldest version I had on hand that still provided the 'gpsprof' 
script) and I don't see any support for those options.

If anyone knows why that might have been a mistake, please let me know.
