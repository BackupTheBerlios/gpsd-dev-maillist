Hello,

my patch providing Ntrip support for gpsd is now more or less ready. Any
feedback is welcome!

For those unfamiliar with the protocol, Ntrip is a streaming protocol
for delivering DGPS correction data over the internet, standardized by RTCM.

If you want to try out Ntrip, you can apply for a free account at
http://igs.ifag.de/index_ntrip.htm. Most streams are from Europe, but
some data sources are located outside Europe, mostly in North America.
