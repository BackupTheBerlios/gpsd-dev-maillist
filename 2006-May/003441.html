This patch just prevents gpsd from trying different port speeds. If
you know you will only ever need to run at 4800bps, you can set that
at compile time. You might want to compile with profiling turned on
(gcc -pg) to see what's sucking up all your CPU. I could do that, but
the problem could very well be platform specific.

Just for reference, gpsd doesn't burn a huge amount of CPU on my zaurus.
