>        ? 202.0400   15.716          ?             ?        ?        ?

> Is the client supposed to combine these three separate lines?

No, it isn't.  The problem with trying to combine these sentebces is twofold:

(1) It can introduce up to one second of latency in the report(s)

(2) In general, with an NMEA device, we don't know where end-of-cycle is,
so we wouldn't know where to stop buffering and ship the accumulated report.

If you used to get just one O record per cycle, you were probably
using a SiRF-binary device that only reported one sentence of location
info per cycle.
