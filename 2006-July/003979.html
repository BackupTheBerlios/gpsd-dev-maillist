I had forgotten that the lowest level of the packet driver actually
uses read(2) and grabs data in chunks of up to 196 characters when 
available.  196 > 80, which is the maximum NMEA line length.

Thus, setting ICANON in the NMEA driver wouldn't reduce the expected 
number of system calls.
