Cross posting for all of the AIS folks out there. Bottom-line is that
the US just reserved the two AIS channels for AIS exclusive use (in
Maritime areas anyway).  I didn't dig into the documents deeper to see
who can actually broadcast on those frequencies.  But, I'm sure in true
FCC fashion all you need is a FCC approved transmitter and maybe a VHF
station license.
