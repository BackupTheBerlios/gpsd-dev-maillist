I don't understand PRN 1 in this context, but the others are clearly a "glitch" in the SBAS
hunting scheme.
It tries to look at those sats through solid earth, at an elevation of -91 degrees.
After it has done it's internal calculations the receiver should know it's useless to try,
let alone report them.

Hence the filter.
