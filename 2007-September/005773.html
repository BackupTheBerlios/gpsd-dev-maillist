> generally useless. So remove it."
>

On a slightly related note, embedded users should be sure that they
have a loopback interface. I received one trouble report this morning
that was eventually determined to be the lack of a loopback. Once the
loopback interface was created, gpsd was able to listen and clients
were able to connect... but we did try this patch during testing.
