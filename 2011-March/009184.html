Well, ARM CortexA9 MP is advertized as having coherent cores. Cache coherency should not be the matter for single CPU multicore systems.

We have yet to check that the acceleration coherency port does what is expected for multiple CPU boards, and most likely, it will also depend on how they are linked together... Possibly, we might get a few leftovers from the old "3-stages-pipeline" that all ARM were emulating up to (at least) ARM9... Don't know for Cortexes.
