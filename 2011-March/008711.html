Good morning / afternoon / evening /... Everyone,

I just happened to investigate on a case where, willing to have time reference (as well as geolocalization information) through a GPS device, it turned out that the device we used, the way it is driven, does update the ntpshm infos a few times but finally ends in a condition where the update is no longer done. What happens is that we switch from the generic NMEA driver to the Ubx driver at some point, and the latter updates the segment when decoding one specific message, and not after every message known to update time.

I have a patch that corrects this. The patch is admitedly not perfect, but 100% functional. I guess I should open an issue and then attach the patch to it ? Feel free to tell me if this is the case. I have seen no open case like mine in the bug tracker.
