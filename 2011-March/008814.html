Yep, ideally gpsd would have the fudge set so that the fudge in the
ntpd.conf would be zero.  As a practical matter we can not get there,
but we can get close.

Also remember that the fudge will depend on the speed of you serial
port, so a fudge for each speed is best.

For a good example look at garmin_ntp_offset() in driver_garmin.c
