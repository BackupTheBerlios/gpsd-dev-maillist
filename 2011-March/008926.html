Not just the packaging but the build environment.  I would have
expected server, include, and libs-with-clients or libgps and client
directories.  Or even a split for the libgps and libgpsd.  Each
directory with its own makefile.

Right now gpsd is a stand-alone binary (not dependent on any lib), yet
libtool creates one of those stupid scripts for it and points to
.libs/lt-gpsd.  It needs to do this for clients linked with
libgps/libgpsd.

I don't know if/when we are goign to move to scons, but splitting out
the server, libs, and clients by dependency and major target should be
done in any case.
