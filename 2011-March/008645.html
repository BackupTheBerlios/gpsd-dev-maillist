Hi,

(something for the time after the enxt release, but I want to bring it
up before I forget about it...)
to avoid linking and missing header file issues in the future, I'd like
to replace AC_CHECK_LIB and similar calls in configure.ac by
PKG_CHECK_MODULES / pkg-config. So the question here is: is pkg-config
available on all our supported architectures/operating systems?

Even if automake and friends will be replaced by scons, cmake or
something else, the question is still valid as they should use
pkg-config, too, if possible.

As a sidenote: I'll move on to get rid of libgps.la in Debian. See
http://lists.debian.org/debian-devel/2009/08/msg00783.html for reasons
behind that.
