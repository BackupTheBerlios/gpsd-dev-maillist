It's good to see different use cases.  I updated the interface and documentation a little to make things like this almost trival. Please send me more information on your project and I may be able to help more.
 
perl -e '
use Net::GPSD3 0.13;
my $gpsd=Net::GPSD3->new(host=>shift);
my $poll=$gpsd->poll;
my $sky=$poll->sky;
printf "Reported: %s, Used: %s\n", $sky->reported, $sky->used;
printf "PRN: %s\n", join(", ", map {$_->used ? $_->prn : "-".$_->prn} $sky->Satellites)
' gpsd.mainframe.cx

PRN: 8, 24, -17, 26, 11, 7, -27, 15, 135, 138
