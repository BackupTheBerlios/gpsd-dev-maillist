The EVK is $100 from SkyTraq (minimum order $200, shipping $30)
Basically the module with a board with a standard 2012 USB to serial
converter and backup battery and regulator making it plug and play -
and an active antenna which goes to the connector.  The modules
themselves are $70, but I would go with the evk.  It usually takes 3
days via fedex international anywhere in the world, customs time may
vary.

It comes with config software but one caution - it allows you to set
faster than 1Hz but if it is written to flash the module will be
almost-bricked (I managed to recover mine but it takes a bit of
effort).  They are fixing that right now :).

Their website isn't very informative, but they have several chips
available including ones for RTK.

Contact: info at skytraq.com.tw
