> Also, if I'm using a SBAS/WAAS enabled GPS so it is already correcting
> for the ionosphere (and the status reflects it), will a RTCM stream
> also attempt to correct it or not?  Will it result in a
> double-correction?

I don't know.  That depends on (a) whether your device's GPSD driver
type has an RTCM passthrough method, and (b) how it copes with RTCM data
shipped to its port.

If your device type has no RTCM passthrough method, you certainly will not
get double correction. If it does, the behavior is all down to how the
receiver firmware reconciles over-the-air RTCM with through-the-port RTCM.
