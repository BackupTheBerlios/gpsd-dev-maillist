Good point, but I would prefer keeping it in microdegrees and add
another integer for nano-degrees which would just be zero for the
99.5% of usage where it doesn't have those extra digits of precision
coming in.

64 bit would be an alternative, but most embedded architectures are 32
bits, and get 4-10x slower when going to 64 bit.
