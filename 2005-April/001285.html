In the old days I wrote many programs that used the serial port to
interact with some sparsely-documented external device and I often wanted
to have some datascope functionality in Linux.  However, there isn't any.
Short of building logging/debugging facilities into every program (which
is what I usually did) there is no generic program that you can attach to
a COM port and let it display/log what is going in and out and what is
happening to the status lines.

I made a special cable to be connected between a serial port and a device,
that taps the signals and sends them to one or two other COM ports, but
even for that setup there is little support.  Programs for DOS that
emulated a datascope with such a cable existed in those days.
