If the garmin driver is installed then these 3 dirs exist and are
readable by all:
	/sys/module/garmin_gps
	/sys/bus/usb-serial/drivers/garmin_gps
	/sys/bus/usb/drivers/garmin_gps

So any of those could replace the current method to test for
the existence of the garmin driver.
