Angus Ainslie <angusa at deltatee.com>:
> I have a cheap gps device that miscalculates the checksum about 50% of 
> the time. Here is a patch to turn checksum checking off.

Bletch.  No good.

This option turns of checksumming on *all* devices, *all the time*.
But modern GPses are hotplug devices -- your macchine could see one that
botches checksums and then another that doesn't thirty seconds later.
Or vice-versa.  Ir, since there is multi-session support now, you
could even have a good-checksum device and a bad-checksum one connected
at the same time.

The correct way to handle this might be by recognizing your bogus GPS and
switching off checksums for *that GPS type only*.  Whst type of GPS is this?
