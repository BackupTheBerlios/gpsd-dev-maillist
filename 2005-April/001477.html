Yo Eric!

Here is my current problem:

> gpsd: No USB serial drivers found.

Looks like root is needed to read /proc/tty/driver/usbserial.

# ls -ld /proc/tty/driver
dr-x------  2 root root 0 Apr 29 14:17 /proc/tty/driver

So even thous /proc/tty/driver/usbserial is 444 I can not read it.

Not much I can do about he perms in /proc.

The garmin driver reads that file to avoid jamming binary down the
throat of an NMEA gps then having a long timeout for the expected
response.  Other early detection methods were suggested but this
was the most universal.  Unless someone has a better way to detect
the driver presence I'll just have to try to send the binary data and
look for the respons.
