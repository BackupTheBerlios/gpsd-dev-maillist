With Oleg's correction to the interpretation formula, at 75W/40n:

gpsd: Height above MSL: 194.380000
gpsd: Height above ellipsoid: 160.838440
gpsd: Height with interpolated correction: 126.942988

That's a geoid separation of -33.895452

According to <http://earth-info.nima.mil/GandG/egm96/intpt.htm> my
geoid separation is -34.04 Meters; .5m is reasonable as an
interpolation error and way below the rest of the factors in the GPS
altitude error budget.

It looks to me like packet 42 MSL altitude is unreliable.  I think I'm
going to have to start discarding that packet entirely.
