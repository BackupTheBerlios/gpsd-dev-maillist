True.  I've done other things to cut footprint in that case.  You'll
note that the Zodiac and Tripmate code have their own conditional-
compilation symbols.  Right now they're always on, set by AC_DEFINE;
I'll make them real feature options soon.  That will trim off 5K of
code.

I also plan to provide a way to condition out everything but the vanilla 
NMEA driver.  That might knock off as much as another K.
