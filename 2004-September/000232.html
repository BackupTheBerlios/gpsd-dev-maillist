> What sentences does it emit?  Is there a link to an online manual?
> Does the chipset have any name or identification other than FV18?

I've attached a patch against trunk with my suggestions for change to
hardware.html for the FV-18 module.  I've checked the sentence list
against the manual, changed the model name to include the hyphen, and
pointed out that the interface is TTL level RS-232, not ordinary RS-232.

I've tested it with 1.98 now.

I've also asked San Jose Navigation if they'd like any of their other
modules "qualified and certified for use with Linux 2.6.8 and gpsd 2.0".
