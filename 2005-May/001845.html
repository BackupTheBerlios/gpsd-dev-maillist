Using something like sirfmon or sirfdemo, you can query the firmware version.

Firmwares can be upgraded, but you're going to have an interesting
time finding a firmware image just laying around. I haven't seen any
official ones posted anywhere, and I'm scared to apply some of the
other OEM firmwares.

It's very easy to destroy your receiver with a bad flash: I ruined 2
pharos igps360's to learn that lesson.
