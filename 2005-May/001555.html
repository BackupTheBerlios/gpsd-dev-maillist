I don't know what exactly you want, but /dev/tty may not be what you want.
See tty(1) and tty(4).

I am getting the feeling that what is attempted here for convenience, is 
neither portable, nor ... convenient?

I have not thought about this too much, but I think this a half solution at 
best.

Having a root process change user/group based on a opaquely chosen file seems 
exploitable. Admins do not expect that.
