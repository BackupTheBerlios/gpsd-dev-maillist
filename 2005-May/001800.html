There is one small problem I have been having with the EC-10X for a while.
Every once in a while it loses lock and only a power off will fix it.

Here is the failure sequence:
	1. start EC-10X
	2. start gpsd
	3.  time passes and alll is working
	4. several days later, EC-10X lcd says 3D fix, but NMEA from it
           says no fix.
	5. power cycle EC-10x.
	6. gpsd hangs in autobaud.
	7. break out of gpsd
	8. restart gpsd
	9.  returns to normal

Here is the reset:

gpsd: <= GPS: $GPRMC,202757.00,A,4405.563,N,12118.393,W,000.0,000.0,200505,0.0,E*4a

gpsd: closing GPS=/dev/ttyS1
gpsd: opening GPS data source at '/dev/ttyS1'
gpsd: no probe matched...
gpsd: hunting at speed 9600, 8N1
[... hang....]
^C
