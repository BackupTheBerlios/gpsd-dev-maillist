Yo Lonnie!


On Fri, 6 May 2005, Lonnie Mendez wrote:

> #define PROTO_TTY "/dev/ttyS0"
>
> That works fine on linux, but you won't find that node on darwin or bsd.
> I propose chaning this definition to "/dev/tty", as this seems to have the
> same group set as the serial nodes in what I have seen so far.  Anyone?

At least on Slackware, or any distro that uses MAKEDEV, the devices
/dev/tty and /dev/ttyS0 are not on the same group.  MAKEDEV puts
/dev/tty in root.tty and /dev/ttyS0 in root.uucp.  Since we are
looking for the group that would fail on Slackware and other distros.
