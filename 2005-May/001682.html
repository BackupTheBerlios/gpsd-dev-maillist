> Just out of curiosity, why does SHM get the highest priority?

The GPS receiver is considered to be a local reference clock and it seems
like NTP believes it, even though 3 other voters decice otherwise.

When you worry about your clock running incorrectly during these
experiments you could temporarily add this to the ntp config (below the
other two lines for the gpsd clock):

fudge 127.127.28.0 stratum 5

This stratum value is the "distance" from the reference clock, and ntpd
tends to favor the lower values.
