Note that the precision of this value is often very questionable.
For Remco, it is off by more than 600ms.  When this would be typical for
all receivers we could add it as an offset when calling ntpshm_put, but I
have seen reports that for some receivers the actual offset varies by up
to 1 second.
Using this for timekeeping is only good when you have no other reference,
and do not care about small errors.
