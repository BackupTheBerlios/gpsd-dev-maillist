The attached patch swaps the East and West markings on the satellite
display of the "gps" Motif application, because they seemed to be wrong.

I was running a GPS module indoors against a West facing window, with
corrogated iron roof, no other structures within cooee, and all the
left-hand satellites shown on the display were green and synchronised.  

I checked against my handheld GPS and it showed the same pattern.  The
compass bearings of the satellites were between 180 and 360.
