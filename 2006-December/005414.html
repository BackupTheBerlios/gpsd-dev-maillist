The uvcvideo project has been having similar problems and they are
tracing it to sending ACKs to quickly.  When you added usleep() before
the ACK you had good results, but after the ACK not so good.  So this
looks similar.  I added a usleep(300) just before the ACK to see if that
helps in our case.  The patch is in SVN.  Please check it out and see if
this helps.
