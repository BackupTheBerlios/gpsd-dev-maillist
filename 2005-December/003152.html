Not too many people want to admit they remember Motif.
Looking at the particular file patch:

--- xgpsspeed.c.orig    2005-11-13 20:02:18.749331651 +0100
+++ xgpsspeed.c 2005-11-13 20:02:37.875855463 +0100
@@ -9,6 +9,7 @@
 #include <X11/Shell.h>
 #include <X11/Xaw/Label.h>
 #include <X11/Xaw/Paned.h>
+#include <Xm/Xm.h>
 #include <Xm/XmStrDefs.h>
 #include <Tachometer.h>

This will not be compiled, if Motif is not present.
A Motif header is already included (XmStrDefs.h), so including another one
does not change anything.

It is safe to do this, it will only lead to problems if your Motif 
installation is hosed, in which case you have bigger fish to fry.
