To make it short: Garmin GPS 76 S runs quite well with gpsd 2.13

Software
· gpsd 2.12 (Debian Sarge)
· Kernel Linux 2.6.8 (Debian Sarge)


Hardware: 

· Garmin GPS 76 S ("Software Version 3.70")
· USB-Serial converter with PL2303 chipset



Since "gpsprof -f cycle" gave an error ("gpsprof: no such formatter")
I connected via telnet to gspd and captured some words: here they are:

,----
| GPBOD
| GPBWC
| GPGGA
| GPGLL
| GPRMB
| GPRMC
| GPSD
| GPVTG
| GPXTE
| HCHDG
| PGRME
| PGRMM
| PGRMZ
`----
