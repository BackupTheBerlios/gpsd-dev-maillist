   I'd be scared to rule out testing certain combinations of serial  
parameters.  Vendors have a nasty habit of breaking firmware in  
subtle ways.  Rather than ruling out unlikely combinations, how about  
a mechanism for re-arranging the order in which probes are tried  
based on expectations?  That way, if Garmin suddenly starts shipping  
something that talks 7O1, we won't simply ignore it because "it can't  
do that".
