Hi Eric,

It's been so long I no longer have any of those units but as I recall I
think the DB9 cable will connect to your serial port directly the
connector labeled gps screws into the gold connector coming out of the
black box.

You can ignore the AMPS connector that connects to a special radio. The
antenna is a dual antenna thats all.

There should be a standard power connector that a Radio shack wall wart
will connect to. You'll need a 5VDC and all RS connectors reverse so
they can be + in the center or - in the center.

Wish I could help more but I really don't remember the unit that well.

Cheers,

David
