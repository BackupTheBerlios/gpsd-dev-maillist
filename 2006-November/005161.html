> there is an inconsistency between PRN display and the skyview. in the
> PRN display numbers as "%d", and in the skyview they're "%03d".

Yes, I know. The default Gtk2 "renderer" for integers in a 'list store' (like 
the satellite table) does it without leading zeroes, as one might expect. The 
use of leading zeroes in the Satellite Geometry view mimics the existing 
Motif 'xgps' app.

Thanks for bringing this up. It's one of many issues that I had intended to 
resolve before final release. I need to hear what everyone thinks -- should 
it always be with or without leading zeroes in the PRN? I'd (mildly) vote for 
leading zeros everywhere.

> I'd prefer to seen "n/a" as "N/A" ... but that's just me.

Taken straight from the Motif 'xgps' code. Happy to change to match consensus. 
I have no strong opinion on this one.
