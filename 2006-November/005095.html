getting people to read configure output is a much harder task.  Notice that
mags like Linux Journal have been harping on this for a while: "You do

levels. Consider the sum total of all the goodness in all the arguments
to all possible users versus the one-time hit to on developer of putting
the man pages back in the SVN.
