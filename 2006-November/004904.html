For now, I'm going to treat itrax hardware and italk protocol support
as hopelessly and unusably broken. I seem to be the only person who
cares at all about itrax/italk - or I'm the only one with an itrax
receiver to test with - so they won't be supported until I write the
code.

I'm going to set them both itrax and italk to disabled in configure.ac
- we don't have a decoder for italk yet, and itrax receivers boot up
as generic NMEA devices anyway. From that perspective, this doesn't
break anything for end users. This also prevents users of 2.34 from
expecting itrax features to work.
