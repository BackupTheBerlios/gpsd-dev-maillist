> Where do I go to buy a "Regulated 5VDC Positive Center" power supply?
> Are they likely to have them at the local Radio Shack?
 
Eric, do you know how much power is required by your black box? You can get 
5V DC from USB cable but USB has limit 0.5A, it can supply maximum 2.5W (0.5A
* 5V). Other 5V DC source is at PS/2 keyboard or mouse connectors; there is
some small limit how much power you can get from there. I use PS/2 mouse
connector to power my GPS. Or you can open your PC and you can get 5V DC from
there (power cable for FDD/HDD/CDROM has 5V and 12V; 5V is between black and
red wire, red wire is positive); this power source can supply much more power
than USB. It is good idea to use some cheap refubrished PC for these power
experiments ;-)

Good Luck!
