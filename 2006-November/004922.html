On Sat, 4 Nov 2006, Andreas Stricker wrote:

> The patch below is against a slightly outdated revision because
> I can't access anonymous svn at the moment.

In princiapl I like where the is going.  Got a nit to mention.

> +<?xml-stylesheet type="text/xsl"
> +  href="http://docbook.sourceforge.net/release/xsl/current/manpages/docbook.xsl"?>

Unless you have this xls installed in your catalog this will trigger a
download from sourceforge for the template.  This will be a problem for
anyone developing w/o a live internet connection.  Then SF can be slow,
unreachable, or just plain down and that hangs the make.  I would prefer
that any dependencies be kept local.

An example is that last weekend I coded for 5 hours in a car.  I was out
of Sprint coverage so no internet.  This would have hung my make until the
network timeout.
