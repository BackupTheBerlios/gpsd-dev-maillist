Petr Slansky <slansky at usa.net>:
> Do you think that TrueNorth is the only GPS that supports digital compass?
> What about future devices? Why do not to support compass on more general
> level? Like to add a flag to gpsd that a device has compass support and to
> add/extend message with compass information. Is this possible?

Yes, but not a good idea.  The number of such flags could proliferate
like crazy.  (Does it report bathymetric stuff?  Does it report
pseodoranges? Etc.)

Dispatching on driver type is, I agree, a little klugey.  But since the set 
of driver types at any given time will be known and relatively small, it's
the best alternative I can see.
