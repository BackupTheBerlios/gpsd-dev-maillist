> Oh, you mean you want a user-initiated baud/parity/stop-bits change to

Garmin Serial Binary is only 9600N1.  So when I switch to that mode I
can jump directly to that rate, bits and partity.  Garmin Serial NMEA is
only 8N1.  After the change to NMEA mode the speed will be unknown but
we can accelerate autobaud up by skipping 7N1, 7N2, 7E1 and 7O1.
