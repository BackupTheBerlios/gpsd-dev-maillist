On Tue, 7 Nov 2006, esr at BerliOS wrote:

> +    case 2:
> +	/* probe for Garmin serial GPS */
> +	/* first turn off garmin binary
> +	(void)gpsd_write(session, "\x10\x0A\x02\x26\x00\xCE\x10\x03", 8); */
> +	(void)nmea_send(session->gpsdata.gps_fd, "$PGRMCE");
> +	break;

Please do NOT do this!

1. Without a long pause, followed by an autobaud to the new speed, the
   $PGRMCE will be missed by the Garmin GPS.

2. It would only work on Garmin serial units, not Garmin USB units.

3. Most using Garmin Binary do not want to leave Garmin binary
