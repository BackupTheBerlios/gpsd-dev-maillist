Chris Kuethe reported a core-dump in gpsd.  Seems if you SIGINT it while 
packet-sniffing (like with Ctl-C to the terminal of an instance held in
foreground by -N), it goes toes-up with session nonzero but session->context
equaling 0x00000001.  Yes, 1.

I reproduced it by running ./gpsd -n -N -D 5 /dev/ttyS0 and hitting two
Ctrl-Cs while it was spewing "gpsd: packet sniff finds type -1" messages.  

Then, in order to sic gdb on it, I reconfigured with --disable-static.
Went through the same procedure...and the core-dump failed to reappear.

Would others please try to reproduce under both static and dynamic linking,
and tell me what they see?
