I've never heard of it.

after reading the article it doesn't seem like the world's worst idea
to generate GPX from NMVL or vice versa. having said that, i don't
think it's part of gpsd's job to generate a huge number of different
formats; that's actually counter to gpsd's mission.

you might want to take this up with the GPSBabel project - their
software aims to convert any log format with any kind of market
penetration into any other. they can do translations like nmea->gpx,
gpx->kml, kml->gpx ... i think GPSBabel would be a more appropriate
program for handling NVML.
