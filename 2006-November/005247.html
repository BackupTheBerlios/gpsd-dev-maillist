I know several easy solutions for this problem:
1) unplug your GPS from PC/device running gpsd
2) power off PC/device running gpsd
3) use an axe to cut cable between GPS and PC; this is not applicable to GPS
operating over bluetooth

In general you have to prevent gpsd from sending command in revert calls to
your GPS. LOL

Seriously, I think you have valueble point and I don't see reason why to
introduce revert calls to gpsd once gpsctl ultility was designed.
