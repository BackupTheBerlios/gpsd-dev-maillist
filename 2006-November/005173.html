Hmmm... Not totally. True, I'm not MCO, but I often try to think like one. And 
although I could make intelligent (?) guesses about the satellite display at 
your site (recent image attached), I'm not really seeing how that display is 
any more intuitive (color-wise and satellite icon-wise) than mine. 

In my app, I see the '## dB' and it's colored so I _know_ the satellite 
strength and can make a good guess what the red/black color of the text 
means.

In your satellite presentation, I suspect that the color of the orb reflects 
the signal strength, but why is PRN #2 bright green when the others are 
darker green? And why do some orbs have a non-filled outline? Is that an 
indication of "part of nav solution"? And why is there an orange-ish, 
non-filled orb (PRN #135)? Is that a WAAS bird? Not trying to "play dumb" 
with this; I just think that it's easy for anyone to consider their design 
"intuitive" and others, well, not so much. :^)
