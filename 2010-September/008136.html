GSPD Folks,

>    I am faced with the task of collecting
> NMEA or raw binary data (e.g.,
> the old "R=2" command) from GPSD via the libgps client
> API.

Has anyone tried the "fanout" driver at http://www.linuxtoys.org/fanout/fanout.html to split GPS serial port data?  I saw an article on it in the Linux Journal and it sounded like a perfect match.  You could split your NMEA data then feed one stream into GPSD and still get another stream from the fanout driver.  In fact, you could run a GPDS v2 daemon and a GPSD v3 daemon on the same serial stream.
