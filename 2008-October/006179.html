xgpsspeed shouldn't need motif at all because it's an Xaw program.  The
following bug has a patch to use Xt contants instead of Xm constants,
and use an Xt header to get them.

http://developer.berlios.de/bugs/?func=detailbug&bug_id=14706&group_id=2116

The patch may be munged in bugzilla (I didn't find 'attach file') so
here it is also:
