You didn't provide a documentation patch for gpspipe.xnl.

The concept is good, but the design violates good practice by changing
the invocation syntax of gpspipe in a way that is not backwards-compatible.

I would be more relaxed about this if gpspipe were normally invoked by
humans who would quickly notice a usage-error message or other problems.
But gpspipe is designed for use in scripts, putting a higher premium
on never causing silent breakage.

Please rewrite with a separate option to change the time format, and
include documentation in the next patch or bundle you send.
