Once again, there are MANY flavors of Garmin, and thus many separate
drivers for Garmins, so best to be specifc that your issue is with the
Garmin Simple Text driver, not with the other Garmin drivers.  The
Garmin Simple Text is not compiled in by default so you should only
have a problem if you override the defaults.
